About Columbia Baltimore Annapolis Visit

Home to the Baltimore Ravens, the city takes pride in its passionate sports culture, and the Ravens, an integral part of the community, contribute to the city's lively and spirited atmosphere. With their hopes set on a superbowl this year, the Ravens have taken the NFL by storm. Lead by their MVP calibar quarterback, Lamar Jackson. Beyond the stadium, Baltimore's nightlife pulsates with energy, offering a diverse array of entertainment options from lively bars to music venues from Federal Hill to the Power Plant. As of 2020, the average income of Baltimore is $51,000 which is only slightly more than the state average of $41,500.
